What is Mavic 2 Pro?

The Mavic 2 Pro drone is an engineering marvel ideal for aerial photography. Equipped with all the best DJI technology, the drone will transform the world of aerial photography.

The Mavic 2 Pro features the all-new Hasselblad L1D-20c camera. The L1D-20c is powered by Hasselblad's unique Natural Color Solution (HNCS) 5 technology, which allows users to capture stunning aerial images with 20 megapixel resolution and stunning colors.

Dlog-M 10

The Mavic 2 Pro supports a 10-bit Dlog-M color profile with a wider dynamic range for more color correction.

CMOS sensor 1

HDR video
